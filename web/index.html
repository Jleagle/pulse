Privacy Policy

At Pulse, your personal health privacy is our highest priority. We engineered a 100% stateless architecture to ensure your sensitive Google Health metrics remain under your absolute control and are never stored in databases or shared with third parties.

1. 100% Private & Stateless Guarantee

No databases, hard drives, or cloud data lakes. Your health metrics are processed entirely in temporary server memory during your active browser session. Once you close your tab or sign out, no trace of your data remains.

2. Zero Data Selling & Limited Use

We never sell, rent, monetize, or share your Google user data with advertisers, data brokers, or AI training models. Pulse adheres strictly to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including Limited Use requirements.

3. OAuth Scopes Requested

We request read-only scopes strictly to generate your visual charts: googlehealth.sleep.readonly, googlehealth.health_metrics_and_measurements.readonly, googlehealth.activity_and_fitness.readonly, and standard profile/email identity scopes.

4. Instant Revocation

Disconnecting from your dashboard or revoking permissions via your Google Security settings immediately invalidates all OAuth tokens.

Terms of Service

By accessing or using Pulse, you agree to be bound by these Terms of Service.

1. Educational & Informational Use Only

Pulse is a visualization tool designed solely for personal wellness tracking and educational purposes. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always seek the advice of your physician or qualified health provider with any questions regarding a medical condition.

2. Stateless Service & Availability

Because Pulse operates in a 100% stateless environment without local storage, service continuity depends on Google Health API availability and your active OAuth tokens. We are not responsible for third-party API rate limits or downtime.
