2. Service Description & Stateless Operation

Pulse is an on-the-fly, stateless health dashboard that visualizes personal health metrics (such as sleep stages, heart rate variability, resting heart rate, and daily step counts) retrieved from Google Health API endpoints.

  • Stateless Processing: As detailed in our Privacy Policy, Pulse operates without persistent database storage. The Service fetches your biometric data in real time upon request and processes it exclusively in temporary volatile server RAM.
